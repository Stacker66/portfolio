About Me



After a 22-year career on the managerial side of the telecommunications industry, I have moved into a field that is totally foreign to me. My realization that changing times require different skill sets, I have thrown myself into web development. It is a simple choice: move forward or be left behind. I am choosing the former instead of the latter.


I am a lifelong Clevelander. When I am not working on coding, I am still not far from my laptop. In the past five years I have edited two books on the history of the Cleveland Indians (Pitching to the Pennant: University of Nebraska Press, 2014, and The Sleeping Giant Awakes: SABR Publcations, 2019). I have authored a third book as well: (So You Think You are a Cleveland Indians Fan? Sports Publishing, 2018). I have contributed to 30 other books on the history of baseball.
